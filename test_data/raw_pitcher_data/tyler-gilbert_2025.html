Tyler Gilbert Bio

  • Fullname: Tyler Gilbert

  • College: Southern California

Tyler Gilbert
Married Caitlin … has a son: Krewe...Attended Santa Barbara (Calif.) City College and the University of Southern California … went 5-2 with a 2.79 ERA (21 ER/67.2 IP) and 66 strikeouts in 22 games (six starts) with the Trojans as a junior in 2015...Graduated in 2012 from San Lorenzo Valley High School in Felton, Calif...Enjoys surfing, skating and making music … plays guitar, drums and piano.

2024

Spent the majority of the season with Class AAA Louisville in the Cincinnati organization and Class AAA Lehigh Valley in the Philadelphia system … also made six relief appearances over two stints with the Phillies...Began the season with Louisville after signing with the Reds on 1/16 as a minor-league free agent with a Spring Training invite...Was traded to the Phillies on 5/7 in exchange for cash...His contract was selected by the Phillies on 8/17 … made his only appearance on 8/23 at Kansas City (1 ER/2.0 IP)… was optioned to Lehigh Valley after the game...Was recalled on 9/9 … allowed two runs over 6.1 IP in five outings to end the season...Threw 11.1 consecutive scoreless IP with the IronPigs from 6/12-7/11, striking out 18 batters.

2023

Spent most of the season at Class AAA Reno...Made all 11 of his appearances for the Diamondbacks between 7/8-8/16.

2022

Made eight appearances (seven starts) in five stints with Arizona and went 0-3 with a 5.24 ERA (20 ER in 34.1 IP)...Increased his fastball (88.0), off-speed (81.7) and breaking ball (76.2) velocity from 2021 (87.9/80.1/75.5)...Owned a 2.20 ERA (4 ER in 16.1 IP) @ Chase Field in four games (three starts)...Placed on the 15-day injured list on July 27 (left elbow sprain)…transferred to the 60-day injured list on Aug. 3...Appeared in 11 games (10 starts) with Triple-A Reno and went 4-4 with a 7.57 ERA (37 ER in 44.0 IP).

2021

Made nine appearances (six starts) with Arizona and went 2-2 with a 3.15 ERA (14 ER in 40.0 IP), including a no-hitter in his first Major League start... His contract was selected from Triple-A Reno on Aug. 3…made three relief appearances (1 R-0 ER in 3.2 IP) before moving into the starting rotation... Placed on the 10-day injured list on Sept. 13 (left elbow fatigue) and missed the remainder of the season... Began the season with the Aces and was 5-2 with a 3.44 ERA (20 ER in 52.1 IP) in 11 games (10 starts) before his promotion.

2020

Did not play due to no Minor League season...Traded to Dodgers for OF Kyle Garlick on Feb. 15…was a non-roster invitee to Spring Training with the Dodgers.

2019

Was 2-4 with 2 saves and a 2.83 ERA in 36 games with Triple-A Lehigh Valley...On the injured list twice: April 7-23 (strained left groin) and May 17-June 20 (strained left hamstring).
